About The Sardinian Disruption

The information held on Sardinia’s landscape will be very disruptive for science. Neolithic farmers crafted meticulous images of the Peratt environment inside the Domas di Janas’ that depict the Z-pinch aurora which passed over the island. Massive plasma connections, called Peratt events, and smaller electrical strikes, called Sipsey events, occurred between earth and the island, instantly sculpting the landscape. In response to the Peratt environment, islanders built the nuraghi. These same forces destroyed the Megafauna. If the lesson of Sardinia is ignored and the Z-pinch aurora return, the location where they descend will be quickly returned to the stone age.
